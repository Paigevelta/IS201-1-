Sea Turtles

The Guardians of our Ocean

Where They Live

Sea turtles can be found in oceans all over the world, mostly in warm and temperate waters.

Why Sea Turtles Matter

  1. They maintain healthy seagrass beds and coral reefs
    • Support biodiversity
    • Keep jellyfish populations in check
    • Balance marine food webs
  2. They support coastal economies
  3. They help clean up the ocean
